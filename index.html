About Avouta's Salon

Yvette - nicknamed Avouta - is a friends and family hairdresser with over 20 years of experience. She's been gifting her clients with a smile every time they get off the chair. Avouta is experienced not only with women but with men as well, making it a welcoming opportunity for all! From infants to seniors, everyone is welcome.

Located in the Scarborough area, her salon creates a home-like atmosphere. Complemented with top-of-the-line tools, you can rest assured that her priority is your comfort.
